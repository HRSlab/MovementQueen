My Journey to Mindful Movement

Drawing from personal experience and overcoming adversity, Tina developed a deep understanding of how intentional movement can heal both the body and mind. Her passion is rooted in her own transformation, providing her with genuine empathy and a drive to help clients uncover their own immense inner strength.

She believes that movement is a direct antidote to feelings of being stuck, and her mission is to provide others with the tools to move through their own challenges, both physical and emotional. This isn't just a job; it's a calling to empower others to live a more joyful, fulfilling life through the power of movement.

Expertise & Qualifications

  • ✓ Personal Trainer & Life Coach: Extensive experience guiding clients from all walks of life toward their goals.
  • ✓ Holistic Modalities: Expertise in Yoga, Yoga Breathing, and Thai Massage to support a whole-body approach.
  • ✓ Lived Experience: A powerful, firsthand understanding of overcoming mental and physical hurdles, which informs her compassionate coaching style.
