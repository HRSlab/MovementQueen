The Foundational Program

Master the core principles of the Movement Queen philosophy and build a new, resilient relationship with your body.

What is the Foundational Program?

This program is the perfect starting point for your journey. It's designed to introduce you to the core concepts of mindful movement, helping you move away from conventional fitness that may have led to frustration or injury. We focus on building body awareness and functional strength from the ground up, giving you the tools to move with more confidence and less pain.

Who is this program for?

This program is ideal for individuals who are new to a mindful movement practice, returning to fitness after a break, or seeking a sustainable alternative to high-intensity workouts. If you want to understand your body better and build a strong, reliable base for lifelong health, this is where you begin.

What's Included

  • ✓ Core Philosophy Curriculum: Access to video modules explaining the key principles of our movement philosophy.
  • ✓ Foundational Movement Library: A library of guided exercises to build your practice at your own pace.
  • ✓ Monthly Group Q&A Call: Join a live call with Tina to ask questions and deepen your understanding.
  • ✓ Private Community Access: Connect with fellow members for support and motivation.
